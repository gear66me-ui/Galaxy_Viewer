GALAXY VIEWER
BANNER VOTING

From a childhood dream… to a window into the universe.

As a young boy, I was fascinated by the stars — by the mystery of the universe and the endless questions it inspired.

Years later, after retirement, I found myself asking a simple question:

Why isn’t there an app that lets you explore galaxies freely — to discover how far they are, how old they are, and how they came to exist?

That question became a mission.

Today, I am building Galaxy Viewer, working over 12 hours a day to create an experience where you can explore every known object captured by humanity’s greatest telescopes — including Hubble, JWST, and more.

A place where curiosity meets discovery. Where the universe is no longer distant — but within reach.

Galaxy Viewer will be released on the Google Play Store in the coming months.

De un sueño de infancia… a una ventana hacia el universo.

Desde niño, me fascinaban las estrellas — el misterio del universo y las infinitas preguntas que despertaban.

Esa pregunta se convirtió en una misión.

Un lugar donde la curiosidad se convierte en descubrimiento. Donde el universo deja de ser lejano — y se vuelve accesible.

Galaxy Viewer será lanzado en Google Play Store en los próximos meses.
